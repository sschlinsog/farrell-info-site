Buy any new SawStop Professional Cabinet Saw (either 3.0HP or 1.75HP) and purchasers will be offered the choice of an Overarm Dust Collection Assembly or a PCS Integrated Mobile Base.

NOTE: PCS175 owners who choose the Overarm Dust Collection will still need to purchase the Dust Collection Blade Guard (TSG-DC)

  • SawStop will send upgrade item direct to customer. Allow up to 4-6 weeks for delivery.
  • Completed forms must be submitted to SawStop within 30 days of qualifying purchase date, accompanied by a copy of the sales receipt that includes purchase date and shows saw paid in full.
  • Only specified accessories will be sent. No substitutions or credits.
